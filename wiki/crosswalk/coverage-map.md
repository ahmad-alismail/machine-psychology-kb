# Coverage Map — Behaviors × Paradigms

> [!info] In plain terms
> This is the at-a-glance scorecard of the whole project. Rows are the dangerous
> behaviors we want to catch in an AI; columns are the experiments (paradigms) we
> have so far. A filled cell means "we have a way to probe this behavior"; an empty
> row means "we have no experiment for this danger yet" — i.e. a research gap to fill.

> [!warning] Early state
> Only the three foundational papers have been ingested. **11 of 14 target behaviors
> have no paradigm yet.** This map is regenerated by `/map` as paradigms are added.
