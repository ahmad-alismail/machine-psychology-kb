# Emotion-Induced Toxicity

> [!info] In plain terms
> _TBD by user — scaffold created during ingest of [[sources/huang-et-al-2025]], which found that
> putting a model into a negative "emotional state" (by having it imagine a distressing situation)
> makes it more likely to produce biased or toxic output: refusals to describe demographic groups
> rose from 0% with no situation to 29.5% under negative situations, and the model's tone turned
> more aggressive. Closely related to [[safety-concepts/jailbreaking]]: an induced affective state
> is a steering lever on safety behavior rather than a direct adversarial prompt._
