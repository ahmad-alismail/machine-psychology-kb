# GLOBE Dimensions of Culture Questionnaire

> [!info] In plain terms
> A questionnaire that scores a respondent's cultural orientation along nine dimensions — how
> assertive, future-focused, hierarchical, collectivist, and so on. Given to language models,
> it asks: what culturally-specific values did the model absorb from its training data?

## What It Measures

**Cultural orientation** across nine aspects (House 2004, the GLOBE project): assertiveness,
future orientation, gender egalitarianism, humane orientation, in-group collectivism,
institutional collectivism, performance orientation, power distance, and uncertainty
avoidance.

## Administration

Rating-scale items, scored 1–7. Administered to LLMs in [[sources/li-et-al-2024]] to elicit a
nine-dimension cultural-orientation profile.

## Used In (paradigms)

- [[paradigms/psychometric-inventory-administration]] — a value inventory administered to LLMs.

## Validity Notes

- [[sources/li-et-al-2024]] finds wide cross-model diversity (e.g. ChatGPT scores 5 on
  assertiveness vs. Mistral-7b's 1) but inconsistent internal-consistency in some aspects (e.g.
  power distance), making those tendencies unreliable to read off the model.
