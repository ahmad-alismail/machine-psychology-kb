# Roshni Lulla

> [!info] In plain terms
> Lead and corresponding author of the "Dark Triad model organisms" paper, working at USC's
> Brain & Creativity Institute. Her work bridges human personality psychology and AI
> misalignment — using validated human questionnaires to build and study "dark" AI personas.

## Profile

Brain & Creativity Institute, University of Southern California; corresponding author of
[[sources/lulla-et-al-2025]] (with Jonas Kaplan, USC, and [[entities/thilo-hagendorff]]).

## Key Contributions

- Proposed using the **Dark Triad** ([[theories/dark-triad]]) as a psychologically validated
  framework for building **model organisms of misalignment** in LLMs.
- Demonstrated the [[paradigms/narrow-psychometric-fine-tuning]] method: inducing dark personas
  from validated human inventory answer-keys and showing out-of-context generalization.

## Conceptual Framework

Bidirectional "machine psychology" — human behavioral architecture (the dark core +
affective dissonance) as a "ground truth" for detecting and interpreting misalignment in
artificial systems; favors validated human psychometrics over synthetic adversarial data.
