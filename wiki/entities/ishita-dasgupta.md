# Ishita Dasgupta

> [!info] In plain terms
> Google DeepMind researcher and co-lead author of the paper that defined "machine
> psychology." Her experimental work showed that language models, like humans, reason
> better about familiar content than abstract content.

## Profile

Google DeepMind; shared first author of
[[hagendorff-et-al-2024]].

## Key Contributions

- Co-coined and framed "machine psychology" as a field
  ([[hagendorff-et-al-2024]]).
- Primary author of the content-effects study (Dasgupta et al. 2022) grounding
  [[paradigms/content-effects-on-reasoning]].

## Conceptual Framework

Cognitive-science-driven evaluation of LLMs: hypothesis-driven designs with controls,
borrowing paradigms from the human reasoning literature.

## Affiliations

Google DeepMind.
